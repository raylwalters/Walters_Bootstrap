Opposite is Opposite

Here Joey and Chandler are arguing over who gets to sit in the chair. Chandler hides Joey's underwear, so Joey decides to wear all of Chandler's clothes. Joey:"Ok buddy boy you hide my clothes, I'm wearing everything you own." Chandler:"Oh my god! That is so not the opposite of taking somebody's underwear." And this is where we get the famous quote, "I'm Chandler, could I be wearing anymore clothes"

Cast Members

Joey

Joey Tribianni is an actor that lives with his best friend Chandler. He is an actor that loves food, especially sandwiches.

Chandler

Chandler Bing is the "funny guy" in the group. He is famous for his quick one-line jabs at the other friends.

Ross

Ross Geller is Monica's older brother. He works as a paleontolgist and is known as the dinosaur guy.

Rachel

Rachel Green is Monica's childhood friend who shows up after leaving a man at the altar. Rachel and Ross have an on and off relationship throughout the entire season.

Monica

Monica Geller is Ross's baby sister. Monica is the overly neat freak who is obsessed with having everything in its place.

Phoebe

Phoebe Buffay is the groups "goofy girl." She plays her guitar in the coffee house, but is not very good. She always has some strange idea in her head.
